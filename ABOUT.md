## Nick Yeager

**AI Solutions Architect / Speaker / Tucson, AZ**

Nick is an AI software developer at ScriptSave with over a decade of experience building software in healthcare, insurance, and finance. He co-founded Quoteapro (AngelPad accelerator), did contract work at USAA, and holds a Microsoft Azure AI Engineer certification.

He builds developer tools, contributes to the Claude Code ecosystem, and runs the Tucson Claude Code Meetup to share agent-building patterns with the local dev community.

**Background:** BA in Political Science, University of Arizona
